PetCare

PetCare Website

Technologies Used:
PHP MySQL JavaScript HTML/CSS

A complete Pet Care website designed with a clean and user-friendly interface. The Home page showcases featured sections, while the Pet Care page provides guides, tips, and important dos and don’ts for both dogs and cats.

The Diet page offers curated recipes for adult dogs, adult cats, puppies, and kittens — each recipe includes detailed health benefits such as improved digestion, coat health, and overall nutrition.

On the Adopt page, users can post pets for adoption. Other users can view these posts, see the pet owner’s information, and contact them directly. Users can also edit or delete the pets they posted.

The Grooming page showcases various grooming style ideas for dogs and cats, helping users choose the best look for their pets.

The Vet page displays a list of veterinary clinics around Bulacan, complete with locations and contact details for easy access.

The Admin Panel provides full control — showing total posts, total users, and tools to manage accounts. Admins can ban users, delete adoption posts, and maintain a safe, well-organized community.
